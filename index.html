About

Connecting computation, experiments, and material behaviour.

I am an M.Sc. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Technische Universität Berlin, specialising in structural mechanics, finite element methods, material modelling, and numerical simulation.

At the Federal Institute for Materials Research and Testing (BAM), I work as a Student Research Assistant in Division 8.5 X-ray Imaging. My interests lie at the interface of computational mechanics, scientific machine learning, and imaging-informed material characterisation.

My recent work includes dynamic finite element analysis of recycled polymer railway sleepers, ML-based constitutive modelling of cyclic plasticity, experimental setup development, and in-situ X-ray computed tomography for mechanical testing.
